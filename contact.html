Frequently Asked Questions

Are your excuses guaranteed to work?
We guarantee they're creative! Whether they work depends on your delivery and the gullibility of your audience. Results may vary based on your acting skills.
Do you offer refunds?
Sorry, all sales are final. But if you're unsatisfied, we'll give you a really good excuse for why we can't refund your money.
Can I use the same excuse multiple times?
We recommend variety! Using "my goldfish is having surgery" more than twice might raise suspicions about your goldfish's health insurance.
Do you have excuses for illegal activities?
Absolutely not! We only provide excuses for harmless social situations. For legal matters, consult a lawyer (who probably needs our services too).
How quickly can you deliver a custom excuse?
Our standard turnaround is 24-48 hours, but we offer rush services for excuse emergencies. Additional fees apply for same-day creative crises.
What if someone doesn't believe my excuse?
That's what our "Excuse Defense Package" is for! We provide backup stories, supporting documentation, and even fake witness testimonials (all clearly marked as fiction, of course).
